Key Findings
🔴 9am is the worst hour — highest no-answer AND most-called
115 calls at 9am (18% of volume) yet 55.7% go unanswered — the highest no-answer rate of any hour. Early morning calls (8–9am) account for 32% of volume but deliver the weakest results.
✅ 13–15pm consistently delivers the best reschedule rates (19–24%)
These 3 hours handle only 20% of volume but drive the highest conversion. 15pm is the single best hour at 24.4% reschedule rate.
⚠️ 16pm anomaly — same no-answer rate as 15pm but only 4.4% reschedule
Something at 16pm drastically reduces conversion despite similar answer rates. Worth investigating separately.
