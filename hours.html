When are calls most effective? When do customers not answer?
DE Market 11labs Voice Bot Overbooking Rescheduling W22–W25 · 659 calls with timestamp May–Jun 2026
Key Findings
🔴 9am is the worst hour — highest no-answer rate AND most-called hour
115 calls are made at 9am (18% of all calls) yet 55.7% go unanswered — the highest no-answer rate of any hour. This is the single biggest opportunity: shifting even a fraction of these calls to 13–15pm would significantly improve outcomes.
🔴 8am–9am: no-answer rate 50–56%, reschedule rate only 10–15%
Early morning calls consistently underperform. Combined, 8am and 9am account for 211 calls (32% of volume) but achieve the lowest reschedule rates of the day.
✅ 13pm–15pm: best reschedule rates (22–24%) with lower no-answer
13pm (22.2%), 14pm (19.5%), and 15pm (24.4% — the day's peak) consistently deliver the best outcomes. These 3 hours handle only 131 calls (20% of volume) but drive the highest conversion.
⚠️ 10am is the exception — lowest no-answer rate (33.3%) but low volume (36 calls)
10am has unusually strong answer rates compared to surrounding hours. Worth investigating whether this is a systematic pattern or a sample-size effect given the low volume.
